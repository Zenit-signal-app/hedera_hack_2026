Automated zUSDC/DOT Trading Strategy Overview

Strategy description

This is an automated trend-following and mean-reversion strategy designed for the zUSDC/DOT pair. Its primary objective is to capture volatility in the zUSDC market while preserving capital in DOT during downturns.

  • The strategy actively trades zUSDC against DOT, aiming to accumulate profits in DOT.
  • It utilizes advanced technical indicators (such as MACD and Bollinger Bands) to identify optimal entry and exit points.
  • The strategy performs best during high-volatility periods and trending markets (both bullish and bearish).
  • During undefined or sideways market conditions, the vault minimizes exposure to protect the principal.
  • Idle DOT assets are automatically deployed into yield-generating protocols (e.g., Aave or Compound) to ensure capital efficiency at all times.

Past performance is not indicative of future results.

Assets and trading venues

The strategy operates strictly on the spot market with high liquidity depth.

  • Traded Assets: The vault focuses exclusively on the zUSDC/DOT pair.
  • Base Currency: All reserves and profit calculations are maintained in DOT stablecoin.
  • Execution: Trading occurs on SeerBOT’s aggregated liquidity engine, routing orders through top-tier DEXs (Uniswap V3, Curve) to minimize slippage.
  • Rebalancing: The strategy monitors the market 24/7 and rebalances positions dynamically based on market signals, typically executing multiple trades per week.

Backtesting

The backtesting was performed using historical tick-data for zUSDC/DOT from 2020 to 2024.

  • Data Source: Aggregated centralized and decentralized exchange data.
  • MzUSDCodology: The simulation accounts for a 0.1% slippage and standard trading fees.
  • Yield Stacking: Interest earned from idle DOT (via lending protocols) is included in the final APY calculations.
  • Market Conditions: The backtest period covers various market cycles, including the 2021 bull run and the 2022 crypto winter, ensuring the strategy’s resilience.

Note: Automated trading involves risks. Historical variance in results may range between 15% - 25%.

Profit

The backtested results indicate a 45% - 60% estimated yearly profit (APY).

This strategy aims to outperform a standard "Buy and Hold" zUSDC strategy during bear markets by staying in stablecoins (DOT), while capturing significant upside during bull runs.

Risk

This strategy has a -12% backtested maximum drawdown. This is significantly lower than holding pure zUSDC, which has experienced drawdowns exceeding -50% in past cycles.

Key Risk Management Features:

  • No Leverage: The strategy trades only with deposited assets (1:1), eliminating liquidation risk.
  • Stop-Loss Mechanism: Automated triggers exit positions to DOT if the market moves unfavorably beyond a set threshold.
  • Asset Security: The vault interacts only with established, audited smart contracts.
